Anime Brief

If you wanna to watch Anime for free. Then scroll to the bottom for the anime Section. More Anime will be added in the future. Anime is actually taken from the word animation and has become the word used by the majority of the world. In Japan this art form is called Japanamation, and it refers solely to what is produced in Japan by Japanese Manga-kas (A “ka” is the writer of a Manga, which is a Japanese comic book). The story from the comic book is then produced in animated form for movies and television.
